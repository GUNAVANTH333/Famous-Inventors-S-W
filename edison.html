Incandescent Light Bulb

Thomas Edison is credited with inventing the first practical and long-lasting incandescent light bulb, which revolutionized the way we illuminate our homes and workplaces.

About Thomas Edison

Thomas Alva Edison was an American inventor and businessman who developed many devices that greatly influenced life around the world. He is one of history's most prolific inventors.

Details About His Work

Incandescent Light Bulb: Edison’s work in developing a viable light bulb paved the way for modern electric lighting. His innovations in electric power generation and distribution have had a lasting impact.
