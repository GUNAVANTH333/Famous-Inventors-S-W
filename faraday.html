Electromagnetic Induction

Faraday discovered the principle of electromagnetic induction, which is the basis of modern electrical transformers and generators.

About Michael Faraday

Michael Faraday was an English scientist who made significant contributions to the fields of electromagnetism and electrochemistry. His discoveries laid the groundwork for much of modern technology.

Details About His Work

Electromagnetic Induction: Faraday demonstrated how a magnetic field could induce an electric current, leading to the development of electric power systems.
